## Exploitable Gaps

### Gap 1: Generous Free Tier
**Problem:** Senja caps at 15, Famewall at 10, Testimonial.to is very limited
**Opportunity:** Offer 50-100 testimonials free, or unlimited text with video limits

### Gap 2: Simple Setup
**Problem:** Endorsal "confusing to use", Testimonial.to "confusing video capture"
**Opportunity:** 2-minute setup, 3 steps max: Create form ‚Üí Share link ‚Üí Embed widget

### Gap 3: Fast Loading Widgets
**Problem:** Senja widgets "ruined my website" with slow loading
**Opportunity:** Lightweight, performance-optimized widgets (<50kb)

### Gap 4: Affordable Branding Removal
**Problem:** $20-60/mo just to remove "Powered by" branding
**Opportunity:** Remove branding at $9/mo or include in LTD

### Gap 5: Responsive Support
**Problem:** "Impossible to reach support", "tickets not answered"
**Opportunity:** Fast email support, clear documentation

### Gap 6: LTD Pricing
**Problem:** Most competitors are subscription-only
**Opportunity:** $49-99 lifetime deal like TidyCal proved works

### Gap 7: AI-Guided Collection (NEW - 2026) ‚≠ê
**Problem:** All competitors use basic text boxes or simple question forms. Users stare at blank boxes not knowing what to write.
**Opportunity:** Smart Prompts that guide users through a 4-step conversation (problem ‚Üí solution ‚Üí result ‚Üí attribution), then AI assembles into a coherent testimonial. **No competitor does this.**

### Gap 8: Form Analytics (NEW - 2026) ‚≠ê
**Problem:** No competitor provides visibility into form performance. Users can't see where people drop off, which steps cause friction, or who their audience is.
**Opportunity:** Full funnel visualization showing:
- Step-by-step drop-off rates
- Highest friction points highlighted
- Audience insights (device, location, referrer, time patterns)
- Completion rate trends over time

### Gap 9: Audience Understanding (NEW - 2026)
**Problem:** Form creators have no idea who is filling their forms - what devices they use, where they're from, how they found the form.
**Opportunity:** Rich visitor data collection:
- Device breakdown (mobile/desktop)
- Geographic distribution
- Traffic sources (referrer tracking)
- Peak activity times
- ISP/organization info
